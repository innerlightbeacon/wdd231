Accra Chamber of Commerce

Chamber Site Plan

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a central resource hub for businesses in Accra. It will provide information about local events, networking opportunities, business directories, and promote local shopping. The goal is to encourage economic growth, support local entrepreneurs, and build a connected business community.

Target Audience

  • Small and medium-sized business owners in Accra
  • Corporate professionals and business executives
  • New residents and potential investors or partners
  • General community members interested in local events

Site Goals

  • Increase member engagement by offering helpful tools and event info
  • Attract new businesses and investors to the Accra area
  • Enhance the Chamber’s visibility and credibility as a local business authority

User Personas

  • Maria (Small Business Owner): 35 years old, owns a boutique. Interested in marketing support and networking events.
  • Diego (Corporate Executive): 47 years old, executive seeking partnerships and economic data.
  • Francisco (New Resident): Recently moved in, looking for local businesses, services, and community activities.

Scenarios

  • A business owner visits the site to join the chamber and view membership benefits.
  • A resident wants to register for an upcoming chamber-hosted workshop.
  • A newcomer explores the site for info on the business environment and available services.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #2E8B57 (Sea Green)
  • Secondary Color: #F4A460 (Sandy Brown)
  • Background Color: #F5F5F5
  • Text Color: #333333
  • Font Family: "Roboto", sans-serif

Typography

Headings will use bold, clean fonts like Roboto. Body text will use a sans-serif font such as Open Sans for clarity and readability.

SEO Strategy

  • Use relevant keywords like “Accra business”, “local chamber”, “networking Accra”
  • Embed Google Analytics on all site pages
  • Verify site ownership via Google Business Profile
  • Encourage backlinks from member businesses
  • Optimize images and metadata for performance and accessibility

Wireframes

The layout below guides the homepage design. It will include:

  • Scalable hero image with a “Keynote Tickets” button
  • Sections for current events, weather, and forecasts
  • Business spotlights with logos, contact info, and links
  • Responsive footer with chamber contact and social links
