Site Plan: Ghanaian Street Food Guide

Site Name

Ghanaian Street Food Guide

This name represents a curated guide to popular street foods found in Ghana. It is descriptive, clear, and focused on cultural food experiences, especially for locals and tourists seeking authentic flavors.

Optional domain availability: ghanastreetfoodguide.com

Site Purpose

The site aims to educate and inform visitors about the most popular and beloved street foods in Ghana. It provides details such as ingredients, where to find them, pricing, and cultural context. It will also include a form where users can suggest a favorite food or vendor to be featured on the site.

Scenarios

  • Which popular street foods should I try on my first visit to Ghana?
  • Where can I find spicy kelewele or freshly made waakye in Accra?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #d35400 (Burnt Orange) - used for headings, icons, and active elements.
  • Accent Color: #27ae60 (Leafy Green) - used for navigation links, buttons, and section borders.
  • Background: #fffaf0 (Floral White) - used for the main page background to maintain warmth and contrast.

Typography

  • Headings: Segoe UI or Tahoma – modern, clean font for readability and structure.
  • Body Text: Verdana or Geneva – simple, highly readable fonts for general text.
